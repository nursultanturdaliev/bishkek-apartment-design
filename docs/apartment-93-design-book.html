3. Concept & palette (Концепция и палитра)

Mood keywords: calm, daylit, oak-warm, textured-matte, low-clutter, household-friendly, durable.

Direction: Scandinavian minimalism with Japandi accents (per §5.5). Whites and warm oak dominate; black is used as a thin accent (handles, frames, taps). No high-gloss "marble look" on large surfaces; no classical mouldings or gilded elements.

5. Room-by-room design (По помещениям)

5.1 Entry (5.8.1) (Вход)

  • Floor: large-format porcelain S1, R10 slip, ~600 × 600 mm, dark grout.
  • Grit defence: scenario A — recessed shoe bench/niche (depth 350 mm), prioritised over runner; tiny secondary runner only on rainy days.
  • Transition to oak: flush brass profile, no step.
  • Sockets: 2 outlets at 300 mm AFF (vacuum, charger), 1 above bench for shoe drier.
  • Lighting: ceiling downlight + linear under-bench glow tied to night scene (dim 1–5 %).
  • Coat: short rail near door (3–4 hooks); main wardrobe in hall.

5.2 Hall & built-in wardrobe (5.8.2) (Холл и гардеробная)

Hall design intent (17.0 m², registry №1) — palette per §5.5: matte off-white walls + light natural oak floor; lighting per §5.6: layer 1 cove indirect 2700 K above wardrobe (~100 lux ambient), layer 2 ceiling downlights along centerline, layer 3 plinth micro-points dimmed to ~2 % as the night scene (1–5 % from §5.6), layer 4 accent spot on niche art. Ra ≥ 90 throughout, no flicker. Built-in wardrobe full-height to 2700 mm.

  • Wardrobe: full-height (FFL → ceiling 2700, top closed), depth 600 mm, push-to-open flat fronts P1 + handle-less; segmented for outerwear / shoes (vented) / suitcases / sports / seasonal.
  • Keys / mail mini-zone: shallow niche 80 × 250 × 120 mm with 1 USB-C charging socket inside (children-safe lid).
  • Stroller / large luggage: dedicated 700 mm bay near entry, not blocking egress.
  • Floor: oak engineered W1 after the entry porcelain; profile flush.
  • Ceiling: flat plasterboard, indirect cove on one side + recessed downlights for daytime, micro-points for night.
  • Acoustic intent: heavy bedroom doors with full perimeter seals; soft panel optional on one wall.
  • Mirror: full-height in one location (visual depth, dressing check before exit).

5.3 Open space — kitchen + dining + living + WFH + TV (5.8.3) (Open space)

Open-space design intent (35.6 m², registry №2) — palette per §5.5: matte off-white walls + ceiling, light natural oak floor, oak veneer accent on tall fridge column and open shelf, off-white slab cabinetry with push-to-open. Lighting per §5.6: (1) perimeter cove indirect 2700 K @ ~100–150 lux ambient; (2) continuous backsplash linear 3000 K @ 500 lux on worktop + separate spots over sink & cooktop (no head-shadow); (3) dining pendant 2700 K @ 200–300 lux on table + accent ceiling spots low UGR ≤ 19. Three independent scenes (background / cooking / evening) per §5.3.1.

Refer to the apartment plan in §4 for full dimensioned layout (kitchen run 3400 mm, dining 1800 × 900, L-sofa 3000 × 2300, working aisle ≥ 1100 mm, TV unit 600 × 3000 mm on east wall).

Kitchen block

  • Linear run along the wet wall (A6); upper cabinets to ceiling; integrated extractor with real ducting (no recirculation).
  • Induction cooktop (A4); oven below or column.
  • Sink: large single bowl over stack; pull-out tap.
  • Splashback: sintered stone S2 (matte, easy clean), continuous.
  • Sockets: 5-gang strip above counter at 1100 mm AFF + 1 USB-C; 16 A circuit for cooktop, 16 A for oven (verify with electrician).
  • No island by default — see A1/§5.1; allow a 1500 mm ø rug zone at the sofa instead.

Dining

  • Solid oak table 1800 × 900 mm extendable to 2200 mm for 6–8.
  • Pendant on its own dimmer; centre over table even if table moves.

Living

  • L-sofa, modular, washable linen T1; rug 2400 × 3000 mm under front legs of sofa and coffee table.
  • TV wall (A5): 65" wall mount at 1100 mm centre; sound bar future-proofed by a 10 mm chase.

WFH desk

  • Niche 1400 × 700 mm, side window light if obtainable; backdrop wall in P1; 1 RJ45 at desk; under-desk grommet for cables.

Zoning without partitions

  • Floor: continuous oak W1; porcelain only under the kitchen run footprint (~500 mm projection). Profile flush.
  • Ceiling: thin recessed linear track defining the kitchen/dining/living rhythm.

5.4 Pantry (5.8.4) (Кладовая)

Pantry design intent (2.8 m², registry №3) — uniform jar / basket system, oak veneer accent on lower closed cabinet, doorway LED strip 3500 K (auto-on at door open) for accurate label reading + IP44 ceiling downlight 3000 K for ambient. One switched outlet for vacuum charging per §5.0.

  • Shelves L-shaped, adjustable; one tall slot for vacuum + step-ladder; one drawer block for cleaning chemicals (child-safe).
  • Light: PIR sensor inside + manual override at door; vapour-tight luminaire if condensation risk.
  • Sockets: 2 (vacuum charging, optional router/NAS).

5.5 Guest WC (5.8.5) (СУ 1.6)

Guest WC design intent (1.6 m², registry №4) — monolithic microcement walls calm the small footprint; vertical LED strip beside the round mirror gives Ra ≥ 95 / R9-high face light (§5.6 must) with a single dimmable IP44 ceiling downlight as ambient — no single overhead point as the only source.

  • Wall-hung WC, in-wall cistern; back-lit shelf for towel.
  • Wall basin on slim oak shelf; back-lit mirror (face light).
  • Floor & walls: porcelain S1, large format, minimal grout.
  • Ventilation grille kept fully open; dust filter accessible.

5.7 Bath / utility (5.8.7) (Ванная 4.2)

Bath design intent (4.2 m², registry №8) — palette per §5.5: large-format off-white porcelain walls + warm-grey limestone-look feature wall behind tub + matte beige porcelain floor, off-white slab vanity, brushed-nickel hardware (sparing dark accent only). Lighting per §5.6: vertical LED face strips beside mirror (Ra ≥ 95, R9-high — §5.6 must), IP44 ceiling ambient 2700 K dimmable, IP65 narrow downlight above tub at 3000 K. No single-overhead-only scenario. Waterproofing certification stays with licensed engineer (§3.4).

  • Built-in tub 1700 × 750 mm with removable side panel (service); thermostatic wall mixer; hand shower on slide rail.
  • Stacked washer + dryer in joinery column SW corner with anti-vibration mat; clearance to wall 10 mm; access to filters via removable kick plate.
  • Slim mirror cabinet 1200 × 800 × 120 mm above vanity with internal sockets (RCD-protected by electrician); LED bars at sides for face light (no overhead glare).
  • Floor mild slope to dry-zone drain near W/D; tile R11 in any wet zone.
  • Heated towel rail electric IP44 on east wall, independent switch outside the room.
  • Clear floor area in front of tub ≥ 700 mm; in front of vanity ≥ 700 mm — verify on site.

11. Sun & shade (Окна и солнцезащита)

  • Open space: light-filtering roller / linen sheer + side-stack curtain for evenings; UV reduction on hottest exposure.
  • Parents bedroom: blackout primary + sheer underlayer; full perimeter side returns to remove edge bleed.
  • Children bedroom: blackout + sheer + soft warm night light L-12.
  • Loggias: outdoor-grade shading (vertical strip blind); avoid dark cabinets that overheat in summer sun.

12. Acoustic & comfort intent (Акустика и комфорт)

  • Bedroom doors: heavy leaf (≥ 35 kg); full perimeter seal; threshold drop seal; verify supplier.
  • Soft surfaces: rugs in living and bedrooms, linen curtains in open space, upholstered headboard parents — to dampen reverb without quoting any dB number.
  • HVAC indoor units selected for low night-mode dB(A) — confirm spec sheet at procurement.
  • Washer / dryer column on anti-vibration mat; nightly cycle scheduled away from bedtime.

13. Sustainability & health (Устойчивость и здоровье)

  • Low-VOC paints and adhesives (ask supplier for data sheet, no certificate is invented here).
  • Mechanical extract preserved in wet zones; trickle vents or window restrictors for night airing in summer (verify with HoA).
  • Long-life finishes (oak floor, stone splashback) preferred over disposable laminate in high-wear zones.
  • Filter cleaning schedule for splits added to O&M (per requirements §5.3.1).
